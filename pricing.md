# Pricing & Licensing Framework

Welcome to my software utility and technical tool distribution portal. To maintain full operational flexibility across multiple independent developments, I adopt a dynamic, project-specific pricing model rather than a rigid fixed matrix.

## 1. Dynamic Project-Based Pricing
Because the utilities hosted and distributed here serve diverse technical functions—ranging from lightweight automation scripts to comprehensive cross-platform toolkits—**all exact pricing tiers, subscription options, or lifetime license costs are explicitly detailed on the respective checkout pages hosted by our payment platform.** 

Before completing any transaction, you will see the final, transparent price clearly displayed on the dedicated software procurement screen.

## 2. Customer-Centric 14-Day Refund Guarantee
I stand firmly behind the quality and utility of my software creations. To provide fellow developers and IT professionals with complete peace of mind:
*   **14-Day Risk-Free Trial Period**: If a premium software component or utility binary does not meet your technical expectations or workflow requirements, you are eligible for a hassle-free refund.
*   **Broad Refund Eligibility**: Unlike standard restrictive digital fulfillment rules, refund requests are generally approved with minimal friction within 14 days of purchase—whether due to environment incompatibility, core feature mismatch, or if you simply change your mind.
