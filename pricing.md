# General Pricing & Licensing Architecture

Welcome to the central billing and licensing directory for BruceNCNP technical portfolios. As an independent software developer and software workshop, I maintain a diverse ecosystem of digital tools, ranging from localized developer utilities to enterprise-ready commercial applications.

To ensure pricing transparency and adaptability, cost structures are decoupled by product category and deployment scope rather than a singular fixed framework.

---

## 📦 Product Categories & Licensing Models

The specific price and license metrics for each toolkit are explicitly calculated and dynamically presented on the respective product’s designated Paddle checkout terminal. Our catalog generally falls under three operational frameworks:

### 1. Developer Micro-Utilities & Scripts
*   **Target Scope**: Highly localized automation scripts, configuration wrappers, and technical prototypes tailored for developers and IT power users.
*   **Licensing**: Lifetime Single-Payment (Individual Use).
*   **Pricing Strategy**: Positioned at a low barrier-to-entry entry point, prioritizing rapid community prototyping and seamless local environment optimization.

### 2. Full-Stack Modules & Production Starter-Kits
*   **Target Scope**: Semi-compiled full-stack frameworks, custom Canvas rendering layout engines, tailored AI interaction middleware, and localized data matrices.
*   **Licensing**: Commercial Single-Payment (Per-Project Deployment or Personal Studio Use).
*   **Pricing Strategy**: Mid-tier structural valuation aimed at saving developers hundreds of hours of scaffolding and structural design.

### 3. Commercial Ready-to-Use Solutions & Layouts
*   **Target Scope**: Fully polished, out-of-the-box software distributions, enterprise SaaS blueprints, and modules with comprehensive intellectual property and copyright commercial transfer licenses.
*   **Licensing**: Extended Commercial Licensing, volume scaling, or annual subscription mechanics.
*   **Pricing Strategy**: Higher premium valuation matching industrial SLAs, advanced copyright indemnities, and end-to-end reliability.
