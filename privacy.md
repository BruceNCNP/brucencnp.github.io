# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

## 1. Zero-Knowledge Local Data Processing
I deeply value technical privacy and data sovereignty. All software utilities and system scripts developed and distributed here are engineered to run strictly within your local runtime environment. We do not collect, harvest, transmit, or store any personal telemetry, network traffic logs, or operational data. 

## 2. Third-Party Financial Transactions
All checkout operations, invoicing, and commercial transactions are securely and independently handled by Paddle, our authorized Merchant of Record. We do not have access to, nor do we store or process, your credit card credentials, bank account numbers, or billing metadata.
